Why it clicks

When you see why, it sticks.

Watch an idea prove itself, from the basics to the big stuff. Once you've seen why it works, you stop memorizing it. You just know it.

a² + b² = c²

The most famous line in math, drawn.

Four copies of a right triangle, rearranged. The leftover space is the proof. No formula to trust, just squares you can watch line up.

= 1

Halve, then halve again, forever.

Add half, a quarter, an eighth, and keep going, and the pieces fill exactly one whole. Infinity, landing on a number you can hold.

= ⅓

A quarter of a quarter of a quarter.

The same trick with quarters settles on exactly one third. Different pattern, same payoff: the moment it clicks.
