Questions

Good to know

Who is Quantica for?

Ambitious learners of any age—students prepping for AMC/MATHCOUNTS, accelerated and homeschool students, and adults who want to learn math properly the first time. If you want depth, not just answers, you'll feel at home.

How is this different from Brilliant?

Brilliant is broad and gentle across all of STEM. Quantica goes deep on mathematics specifically—sequenced from Prealgebra to pre-olympiad rigor, with genuine problem-solving and competition-level depth.

How is this different from AoPS?

We share AoPS's love of hard problems. The difference is the model: AoPS sells live, scheduled courses for $300–950 each. Quantica is self-paced, all-access, and one affordable subscription—learn on your own clock.

Do I need to buy each course?

Never. One subscription unlocks every course and the entire path. No upsells, no per-course decisions.

Is it really free to start?

Yes—the first Prealgebra unit is free, no card required. You only subscribe when you want to continue the path.

My child is under 18—how does that work?

A parent sets up and manages the subscription, with a progress view to follow along. The Family plan covers up to four learners.
