Meet Milo

A tutor on call for every problem.

When a problem stops you cold, Milo asks the one question that gets you moving again — never the answer. His whole job is to make you feel smart enough to find it yourself.

  • Attempt first. The answer stays hidden until you've genuinely tried.
  • One nudge at a time. Small hints toward the next step, so the work stays yours.
  • Meets you where you're stuck. Milo reads what you actually tried, not a script.
01

You reach the concept, then we name it.

Add half, then a quarter, then an eighth, forever. The pieces swallow the whole triangle and infinity quietly settles at 1. You watch it happen, then we give it a name.

02

Milo only nudges — never the answer.

Milo never just hands over the answer — a nudge here, a question there. Tear a triangle's three corners off and they line up into one straight line: 180°. You're the one who spots it, so the idea is yours.

03

It carries to problems you've never seen.

Slice a circle fine enough and it unrolls into a rectangle — and there's where πr² comes from. Understood from the ground up like that, an idea becomes a tool you reach for on problems you've never met.
